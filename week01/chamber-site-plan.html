Overview

Purpose

The Mar del Plata chamber of commerce is an organization dedicated to promoting and supporting the local business community. Our website serves as a resource for our members and the broader community, providing information about our organization, upcoming events, and resources for business owners and entrepreneurs. We aim to provide a platform for our members to connect with each other, share ideas and knowledge, and collaborate on initiatives that benefit the Mar del Plata business community. Our ultimate goal is to help create a prosperous economy in Mar del Plata by supporting the success of our member businesses.

Audience

the target audience for the Mar del Plata chamber of commerce site would be diverse, comprising both business owners and individuals interested in supporting the local business community.

Scenarios

  • How does the Mar del Plata chamber of commerce support new business owners in the community?
  • What resources are available to existing chamber members on the Mar del Plata chamber of commerce website?
  • How can community members get involved in events and initiatives organized by the Mar del Plata chamber of commerce?
